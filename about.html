About studio Ghibli

Ghibli started in 1985 by talented creators Hayao Miyazaki and Isao Takahata. They're famous for making beautiful hand-drawn animated movies with really touching stories. The name "Ghibli" is derived from the Italian word for a warm, Mediterranean breeze, symbolizing the studio's aspiration to bring fresh, creative ideas to the world of animation. You might know some of their classics like "My Neighbor Totoro" and "Spirited Away." These movies are loved all around the world. Ghibli's characters like Totoro and No-Face have become super popular.
